Concept of Business

All the activities related to the production, distribution, selling of goods and services with a profit motive in order to satisfy human needs and wants can generally be defined as business.

There are also businesses which fulfill needs without a profit motive — e.g. Red Cross, Leo Club.

Needs vs Wants

❤️ Needs
  • Compulsory for survival
  • Food, clothes, house, education, health
  • Common to all
  • Essential for survival
✨ Wants
  • Different ways of fulfilling needs
  • Unlimited & vary from person to person
  • Can be created by businesses
  • Subjective

Evolution of Business

Drawbacks of the Barter System

  • Lack of double coincidence of wants
  • Lack of a common measure of value
  • Indivisibility of certain goods
  • Difficulty in making deferred payments
  • Difficulty in storing value

Stages of Evolution

💱 Barter System
First stage — direct exchange of goods for goods
💰 Use of Money
Materials → Metals → Coins & Notes → Bank Money → Electronic Money
🏭 Industrial Revolution
Cottage industries → large scale; growth of banking, transport, insurance
💻 Information Era
E-Commerce & E-Business — internet-based transactions
Commerce = Trade + Support Services
🌐

E-Commerce & E-Business

💻 E-Commerce
  • Buying & selling through internet
  • Only transactions
💻 E-Business
  • All business activities via internet
  • Includes communication, collaboration, etc.

Vision, Mission & Objectives

💡 Vision
Ultimate, inspirational, long-term aspiration. "What do we want to ultimately become?" Ex: Google — "Provide access to the world's information in one click"
🎯 Mission
Core purpose and focus. "Why do we exist? Who do we serve?" Ex: Alphabet — "Organize the world's information and make it universally accessible"
📋 Objectives
Broad, long-term outcomes to fulfill the vision
✅ SMART Goals
Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound targets
