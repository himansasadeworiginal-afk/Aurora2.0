✉ What is Communication?

Communication can be defined as sending and receiving information, understanding and sharing information from one party to another or many parties.

Communication is important for business activities as well as social activities.

⚙ Importance of Communication

  • To exchange ideas between parties at businesses
  • To maintain employer-employee relationship
  • To manage human and physical resources well
  • For marketing promotional activities
  • To exchange ideas and messages socially
  • To know instantly about international incidents
  • To get information for educational activities
  • To know about new inventions with technological improvement
  • To build social relationships nationally and internationally

♻ Communication Process

Sender

  • The party sending the message
  • Speaker/lecturer (verbal) or signer/institution (written)

Message

  • The information the sender provides to the receiver

Media

  • How information is provided
  • Verbal medium, written medium, signs & symbols

Receiver

  • The person who grasps the message
  • Can be a reader, listener, or viewer

Response

  • The way the receiver acts on what they heard or read

Feedback

  • Response from receiver to sender confirming receipt and understanding

♿ Communication Media

Communication media are categorized as: Oral media, Written media, and Signs & Symbols media.

Internal Communication

  • Among managers & owners

  • Among departments

External Communication

  • With customers
  • With other businesses & suppliers
